The Best Movies According to Mathews Manuel

My favourite top 3 movies

1. Platoon

The movie is based on Vietnam world war. You will love the story if you ever had a defence dream at your younger age. The visuals and the background scores gives an absolute feel.

2. The Scouts Guide to Zombies

Ofcourse, it's a zombie movie. The story tells about the survival of a group of young people (Students) from the attack of zombies. The movie has humourous scenes as well. So it's really funny as well as thrilling to watch.

3. James Bond Series

Who doesn't like to watch 007 action. It's always thrilling and fascinating to watch the JB series.
